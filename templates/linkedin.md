## When LinkedIn is worth it

- B2B SaaS (decision-makers are here)
- Career / hiring tools
- Industry-specific products (legal tech, HR, med, finance)
- If you have 500+ connections in target audience

Skip LinkedIn for: indie CLI tools, gaming, meme tooling, ML benchmarks.
