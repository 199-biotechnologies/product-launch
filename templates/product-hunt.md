## Launch checklist

- [ ] Schedule launch for 12:01 AM PT, Tue/Wed/Thu
- [ ] NOT Friday, weekend, or last week of month
- [ ] 20–30 supporters DMed (privately) ≥ 24h in advance
- [ ] Hunter confirmed (if using one — 2026, 60% of #1s are self-hunted)
- [ ] All 5 gallery images + video uploaded and reviewed
